## Executive Summary

This document provides a detailed definition of the "Non-Regulated Product Concept Ideation & Validation" Level 3 (L3) capability within Quantum Energy. This capability is crucial for identifying, assessing, and refining innovative ideas for new non-regulated products and services, ensuring they align with market needs and strategic growth objectives.

---

## 1. Capability Definition

**Capability Name:** Non-Regulated Product Concept Ideation & Validation
**Capability ID:** 11.2.1
**Scope:** Non-Regulated (Advanced Energy Solutions)
**Description:** The capability to generate, explore, and critically evaluate new product and service concepts for Quantum Energy's non-regulated portfolio. This involves synthesizing market insights, technological trends, and customer feedback to develop initial product ideas, and then systematically validating their desirability, feasibility, and viability before committing to further development.

---

## 2. Capability Scope and Boundaries

### 2.1. In Scope

*   Generating a diverse pipeline of raw product and service ideas for non-regulated markets.
*   Conducting qualitative (e.g., focus groups, interviews) and quantitative (e.g., surveys, market sizing) research to understand customer problems and unmet needs.
*   Analyzing competitive landscape and identifying differentiation opportunities for non-regulated offerings.
*   Evaluating emerging technologies and assessing their potential application to new products.
*   Developing preliminary product concepts, including high-level features, target customers, and potential value propositions.
*   Conducting internal and external validation of product concepts with stakeholders and potential customers.
*   Assessing technical feasibility and resource requirements for concept development.
*   Estimating initial market potential and financial viability of concepts (e.g., preliminary business case).
*   Prioritizing concepts for further design and development.
*   Iterative refinement of concepts based on feedback.

### 2.2. Out of Scope

*   Detailed product design and specification (covered by Product Concept & Design - L2: 11.2, and Solution Architecture Design - L3: 11.2.3).
*   Full-scale product development and engineering (covered by Product Development & Engineering - L2: 11.3).
*   Market launch activities (covered by Product Launch & Commercialization - L2: 11.4).
*   Regulated product development (covered by relevant regulated capabilities).
*   Enterprise-wide market trend analysis (covered by Market Research & Analysis - L2: 2.1).

*   **Target Maturity:** (To be defined)
*   **Owning Organization:** Advanced Energy Solutions Business Unit (Product Innovation Team)

## 4. Inputs and Outputs

### 4.1. Inputs

*   Strategic Growth & Market Diversification strategic objectives.
*   Market Research & Analysis reports.
*   Customer Insights & Analytics data.
*   Technology scouting reports.
*   Competitive intelligence.
*   Employee innovation ideas.
*   Partnership opportunities.

### 4.2. Outputs

*   Validated new product/service concepts.
*   Preliminary value propositions.
*   High-level feature sets and requirements.
*   Initial business cases and financial viability assessments.
*   Prioritized pipeline of concepts for further design.
*   Feedback for market research and technology teams.
