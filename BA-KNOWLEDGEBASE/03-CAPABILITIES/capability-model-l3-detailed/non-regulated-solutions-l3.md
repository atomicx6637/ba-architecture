## 2. Non-Regulated Product Development L3 Capabilities

**Parent L1 Capability:** 11. Non-Regulated Product Development (NonReg)
**Definition:** The capability to research, design, develop, and launch innovative utility-supporting and utility-like non-regulated products and services that meet market needs and drive profitable growth.

### 2.1. Parent L2 Capability: 11.2. Product Concept & Design

**Definition:** The capability to ideate, conceptualize, and design innovative non-regulated product and service offerings, translating market insights into compelling solutions.

#### L3 Capabilities:

*   **11.2.1. Concept Ideation & Validation:** Generate new product ideas based on market research, technology trends, and customer feedback, then validate their feasibility and desirability with target segments.
*   **11.2.2. Value Proposition Definition:** Clearly articulate the unique benefits and value that a non-regulated product or service offers to specific customer segments.
*   **11.2.3. Solution Architecture Design:** Develop the technical and functional architecture for new products, outlining components, integrations, and operational workflows.
*   **11.2.4. User Experience (UX) Design:** Design intuitive and engaging user interfaces and customer interaction flows for non-regulated digital products and services.
*   **11.2.5. Prototyping & Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Definition:** Create prototypes and define the scope for minimum viable products to test concepts and gather early feedback.
*   **11.2.6. Business Case Development (Non-Regulated):** Develop comprehensive business cases for new non-regulated products, including market sizing, revenue projections, cost estimates, and profitability analysis.

### 2.2. Parent L2 Capability: 11.4. Product Launch & Commercialization

**Definition:** The capability to plan and execute the successful market launch of new non-regulated products and services, including pricing, branding, and channel readiness.

#### L3 Capabilities:

*   **11.4.1. Go-to-Market Strategy Development:** Define the market entry strategy, target segments, pricing models, and distribution channels for new non-regulated offerings.
*   **11.4.2. Marketing Campaign Development (Non-Regulated):** Design and execute marketing campaigns specific to non-regulated products, leveraging digital, social, and traditional media.
*   **11.4.3. Sales Enablement & Training (Non-Regulated):** Develop sales tools, collateral, and training programs for sales teams and channel partners to effectively sell new non-regulated products.
*   **11.4.4. Channel Readiness & Partner Onboarding:** Prepare sales and delivery channels, including onboarding new partners, for the launch and fulfillment of non-regulated offerings.
*   **11.4.5. Post-Launch Performance Monitoring:** Track key performance indicators (KPIs) immediately post-launch to assess market acceptance, sales velocity, and initial customer feedback.
*   **11.4.6. Regulatory Approval & Compliance (Non-Utility Specific):** Ensure that new non-regulated products and services comply with relevant consumer protection, privacy, or industry-specific regulations outside of traditional utility oversight.

## 3. Non-Regulated Sales & Marketing L3 Capabilities

**Parent L1 Capability:** 12. Non-Regulated Sales & Marketing (NonReg)
**Definition:** The capability to effectively market, sell, and acquire customers for Quantum Energy's non-regulated products and services, building market share and driving revenue.

### 3.1. Parent L2 Capability: 12.4. Customer Acquisition & Conversion

**Definition:** The capability to effectively convert leads into customers for non-regulated products and services, managing the sales pipeline and closing deals.

#### L3 Capabilities:

*   **12.4.1. Lead Generation & Qualification (Non-Regulated):** Identify and qualify potential customers for non-regulated offerings through various inbound and outbound marketing and sales activities.
*   **12.4.2. Sales Pipeline Management:** Manage the progression of leads through the non-regulated sales pipeline, from initial contact to closed-won.
*   **12.4.3. Solution Selling & Customization:** Understand customer needs and tailor non-regulated solutions, often requiring custom proposals or configurations.
*   **12.4.4. Contract Negotiation & Closing (Non-Regulated):** Negotiate terms and close sales contracts for non-regulated products and services.
*   **12.4.5. Customer Onboarding (Non-Regulated):** Facilitate the smooth transition of new non-regulated customers, ensuring successful product activation and initial usage.
*   **12.4.6. Sales Performance Reporting & Analytics:** Track and analyze sales metrics, conversion rates, and sales team performance for non-regulated offerings to identify areas for improvement.
