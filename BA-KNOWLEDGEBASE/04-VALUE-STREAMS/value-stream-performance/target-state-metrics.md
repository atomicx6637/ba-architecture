## Executive Summary

This document outlines the target state performance metrics for Quantum Energy's key value streams, with a focus on achieving our 2030 strategic objectives. These targets represent ambitious yet achievable goals for improving efficiency, enhancing customer experience, driving non-regulated growth, and maintaining operational excellence across the enterprise. They will guide our transformation efforts and provide clear benchmarks for success.

---

## 1. Introduction

These target metrics are derived from our strategic objectives and capability development roadmaps. They reflect a future state where Quantum Energy leverages advanced technology, streamlined processes, and integrated data to deliver superior value in both its regulated and non-regulated business segments.

## 3. General Impact of Achieving Target Metrics

*   **Strategic Objective Achievement:** Directly demonstrates progress and success against Quantum Energy's 2030 strategic objectives, including reliability, customer satisfaction, and non-regulated growth targets.
*   **Enhanced Customer Experience:** Significantly improved digital and human-assisted interactions, leading to higher customer satisfaction and loyalty across all offerings.
*   **Operational Excellence:** Greater efficiency, automation, and data-driven decision-making in core regulated operations and non-regulated service delivery.
*   **Accelerated Growth:** Streamlined non-regulated value streams enable faster market penetration and profitable scaling of new offerings.
*   **Stronger Financial Performance:** Reduced costs, optimized resource utilization, and successful rate case outcomes contribute to robust financial health.
