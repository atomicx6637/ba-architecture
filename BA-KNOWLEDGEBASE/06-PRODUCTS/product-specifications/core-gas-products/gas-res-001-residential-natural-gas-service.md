## Executive Summary

This document provides a detailed specification for Quantum Energy's Residential Natural Gas Service (Product ID: GAS-RES-001). This is a core regulated utility product, defining the standard natural gas delivery service provided to residential customers within our service territory. It outlines the product's features, value proposition, target segments, and the key capabilities required for its delivery.

---

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** Residential Natural Gas Service
**Product ID:** GAS-RES-001
**Regulatory Scope:** Regulated
**Description:** The provision of natural gas from Quantum Energy's distribution network to residential customers' premises for typical household uses (e.g., heating, cooking, water heating, clothes drying). This includes the infrastructure to deliver gas, metering, billing, and associated customer support services, all operating under state and federal regulatory frameworks.

---

## 2. Value Proposition

*   **Reliable Supply:** Consistent and dependable supply of natural gas for home heating, cooking, and appliances.
*   **Safety Assurance:** Delivery through a pipeline network maintained to rigorous safety standards, with responsive emergency services for gas leaks.
*   **Affordability:** Fair and regulated pricing, ensuring access to essential energy for heating and household needs.
*   **Convenience:** Easy access to account information, billing, and customer support.

---

## 3. Target Customer Segments

*   **Primary:** Regulated Residential Customers (Homeowners, Renters, Multi-family Dwelling Residents).

---

## 4. Product Features & Components

*   **Gas Delivery:** Provision of natural gas from the distribution main to the customer's meter.
*   **Distribution Infrastructure:** Connection to Quantum Energy's natural gas distribution network.
*   **Metering:** Installation and maintenance of a natural gas meter to measure consumption.
*   **Billing:** Monthly invoicing for gas consumed and associated charges based on approved tariffs.
*   **Emergency Response:** 24/7 service for reporting and responding to gas leaks or other gas-related emergencies.
*   **Customer Support:** Access to Quantum Energy's customer service channels (phone, digital) for inquiries and assistance.
*   **Safety Inspections:** Periodic safety checks of the utility-owned gas infrastructure up to the meter.
*   **Odorization:** Provision of odorant in natural gas for safety detection.

---

## 5. Delivery Model

*   **Model Type:** Continuous Service.
*   **Contract Term:** Indefinite (as long as customer resides at service address).
*   **Billing Cycle:** Monthly.
*   **Payment Options:** Direct debit, online payment, mail, phone, in-person.
*   **Service Activation:** Typically within 1-2 business days for standard connections.

---

## 6. Rate Structures (Examples)

*   **Standard Residential Rate:** Per-therm or per-Ccf charge, potentially with a fixed customer charge.
*   **Seasonal Rates:** Different rates for winter vs. summer consumption.
*   **Budget Billing:** Optional program to levelize monthly payments.
