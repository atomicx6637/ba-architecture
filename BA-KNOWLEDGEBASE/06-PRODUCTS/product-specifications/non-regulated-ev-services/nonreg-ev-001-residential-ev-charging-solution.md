## Executive Summary

This document provides a detailed specification for Quantum Energy's Residential EV Charging Solution (Product ID: NONREG-EV-001). This is a core non-regulated offering from our Advanced Energy Solutions (AES) business unit, designed to provide electric vehicle owners with a convenient, reliable, and safe home charging experience. It outlines the product's features, value proposition, target segments, and the key capabilities required for its delivery.

---

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** Residential EV Charging Solution
**Product ID:** NONREG-EV-001
**Regulatory Scope:** Non-Regulated (offered by Quantum Energy's Advanced Energy Solutions Business Unit)
**Description:** A complete home electric vehicle charging solution that includes the sale and professional installation of a Level 2 (240V) smart EV charger, integration with a mobile application for charge management, and optional smart charging features designed to optimize charging times for cost savings or grid benefits.

---

## 2. Value Proposition

*   **Convenience & Speed:** Fast and reliable home charging, eliminating range anxiety and saving time compared to public charging.
*   **Cost Optimization:** Smart charging features (e.g., scheduling, integration with time-of-use rates) help minimize charging costs.
*   **Professional & Safe Installation:** Worry-free setup by certified technicians, ensuring electrical safety and compliance.
*   **Trusted Support:** Backed by Quantum Energy's expertise and customer service for peace of mind.
*   **Smart Integration:** Potential for integration with Quantum Energy's Smart Home Energy Management platform for holistic home energy control.

---

## 3. Target Customer Segments

*   **Primary:** Non-Regulated EV Owners (Residential).
*   **Secondary:** Regulated Residential Customers (who own EVs and seek charging solutions).

---

## 4. Product Features & Components

*   **Level 2 Smart EV Charger:** High-quality, Wi-Fi enabled Level 2 charging station (e.g., 32A/40A/48A capable), compatible with all major EV models (J1772 connector).
*   **Professional Installation:** Site assessment, electrical panel upgrade (if required), wiring, charger mounting, and testing by licensed electricians.
*   **Mobile Application Access:** Dedicated app for remotely starting/stopping charges, scheduling, viewing charge history, and receiving notifications.
*   **Smart Charging Features:**
    *   **Scheduled Charging:** Set specific times for charging to take advantage of off-peak electricity rates.
    *   **Load Management:** Integration with home energy management systems to prevent overloading the electrical panel.
    *   **Optional Grid Integration:** Ability to participate in future demand response or grid services programs (e.g., for bill credits).
*   **Dedicated Customer Support:** Access to specialized technical support for charger operation and troubleshooting.
*   **Warranty:** Manufacturer's warranty on charger hardware, installation warranty from Quantum Energy.

---

## 5. Delivery Model

*   **Model Type:** Solution Sale (Hardware + Installation Service), with optional ongoing support plans.
*   **Pricing:** Fixed price for standard installation, variable for complex electrical upgrades. Charger hardware purchased upfront or financed. Optional monthly fee for premium smart charging features or extended warranty.
*   **Distribution Channels:** Direct sales (online, call center), automotive dealership partnerships, developer partnerships.
*   **Installation:** Professional installation required, performed by Quantum Energy certified electricians or authorized contractors.
*   **Customer Support:** Dedicated non-regulated support channels (phone, chat, email).
