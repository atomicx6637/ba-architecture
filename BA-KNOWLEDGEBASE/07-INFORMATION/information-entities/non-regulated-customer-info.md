## Executive Summary

This document defines the key information entities related to Quantum Energy's non-regulated customer base, primarily served by the Advanced Energy Solutions (AES) business unit. This information is critical for managing sales, service delivery, product usage, and customer relationships in competitive energy solutions markets. It focuses on the data required to support non-regulated business operations, distinct from data related to regulated utility services.

---

## 1. Introduction

Non-Regulated Customer Information comprises data necessary to market, sell, deliver, and support non-regulated products and services (e.g., smart home solutions, EV charging, DG/storage). This data often includes granular usage patterns, device-specific information, and preferences that are key for personalization and product development.

## 2. Key Information Entities

### 2.1. Customer Profile (Non-Regulated)

*   **Definition:** Personal and preference information identifying the individual or organization engaging with Quantum Energy for non-regulated products and services. Often linked to a Customer Profile (Regulated) for a unified view.
*   **Attributes:**
    *   `NonReg_Customer_ID` (Primary Key)
    *   `Customer_ID` (Foreign Key - links to generic Customer entity, enabling unified view)
    *   `Marketing_Consent_Flag`
    *   `Product_Interests` (e.g., "Smart Home", "EV", "Solar")
    *   `Preferred_Contact_Method` (for non-regulated marketing)
    *   `Purchase_History_NonReg`
    *   `Subscription_Status` (for recurring services)
    *   `Loyalty_Program_Status` (if applicable)
    *   `Lead_Source`
    *   `Sales_Funnel_Stage`
*   **Relationships:**
    *   One-to-many with Non-Regulated Product Subscription.
    *   One-to-many with Smart Device Data.
    *   One-to-many with Non-Regulated Service Request.

### 2.2. Non-Regulated Product Subscription

*   **Definition:** A record representing a customer's active enrollment in a non-regulated product or service offering.
*   **Attributes:**
    *   `Subscription_ID` (Primary Key)
    *   `NonReg_Customer_ID` (Foreign Key)
    *   `Product_ID` (Foreign Key - links to Non-Regulated Product definitions)
    *   `Premise_ID` (Foreign Key - where service is delivered/installed)
    *   `Subscription_Status` (e.g., "Active", "Pending Installation", "Cancelled")
    *   `Start_Date`
    *   `End_Date` (if subscription based)
    *   `Monthly_Fee` / `One_Time_Charge`
    *   `Associated_Device_IDs` (list of smart devices, EV chargers, etc.)
    *   `Installation_Date`
    *   `Installer_ID`
*   **Relationships:**
    *   One-to-one with Non-Regulated Product.
    *   One-to-many with Non-Regulated Service Request.

### 2.3. Smart Device Data

*   **Definition:** Granular operational data generated by smart home devices, EV chargers, or distributed energy resources installed at customer premises.
*   **Attributes:**
    *   `Device_ID` (Primary Key)
    *   `Subscription_ID` (Foreign Key)
    *   `Device_Type` (e.g., "Smart Thermostat", "EV Charger", "Solar Inverter")
    *   `Manufacturer`
    *   `Model`
    *   `Firmware_Version`
    *   `Operating_Status` (e.g., "Online", "Offline", "Error")
    *   `Last_Reported_Timestamp`
    *   `Telemetry_Data` (e.g., temperature, power draw, charge state, generation output - often stored in time-series database)
    *   `Control_Command_Log`
*   **Relationships:**
    *   One-to-many with Non-Regulated Product Subscription.
    *   One-to-many with Energy Consumption Data (for aggregated device consumption).

### 2.4. Non-Regulated Service Request

*   **Definition:** A record of a customer-initiated request for support, troubleshooting, or maintenance related to a non-regulated product or service.
*   **Attributes:**
    *   `Service_Request_ID` (Primary Key)
    *   `Subscription_ID` (Foreign Key)
    *   `NonReg_Customer_ID` (Foreign Key)
    *   `Request_Type` (e.g., "Technical Support", "Installation Issue", "Billing Inquiry")
    *   `Description`
    *   `Date_Submitted`
    *   `Status` (e.g., "Open", "In Progress", "Resolved")
    *   `Priority`
    *   `Resolution_Details`
    *   `Assigned_Agent_ID`
    *   `Associated_Work_Order_ID`
*   **Relationships:**
    *   One-to-one with Non-Regulated Product Subscription.

## 3. Data Governance Considerations

*   **Privacy by Design:** Implementing privacy-preserving measures from the outset for all non-regulated customer data.
*   **Customer Consent:** Explicit customer consent required for collection, use, and sharing of non-regulated data, especially with regulated utility data.
*   **Data Security:** Robust cybersecurity measures to protect sensitive smart device data and customer behavioral patterns from breaches.
*   **Market Conduct:** Ensuring data practices do not create anti-competitive advantages or violate market conduct rules.
*   **Data Accuracy & Timeliness:** Critical for delivering real-time insights and responsive service for advanced energy solutions.
*   **Data Lifecycle:** Defined policies for data retention and archival, especially for granular telemetry data.
