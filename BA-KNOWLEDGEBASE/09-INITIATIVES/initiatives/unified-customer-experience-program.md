## Executive Summary

The Unified Customer Experience Program (UCE-001) is an enterprise-wide transformation initiative for Quantum Energy, designed to deliver a seamless, personalized, and proactive customer experience across all touchpoints. This program will integrate customer interactions for both regulated utility services and non-regulated advanced energy solutions, ensuring a consistent brand experience, improved satisfaction, and efficient service delivery. It is a critical enabler for achieving our "Customer-Centricity & Digital Engagement" strategic theme.

---

## 1. Program Vision & Goals

### 1.1. Vision

To empower Quantum Energy customers with effortless access to information and services, through personalized, proactive, and digitally-enabled interactions that foster trust and loyalty across our entire portfolio of offerings.

### 1.2. Program Goals

*   **Achieve Unified Customer View:** Consolidate customer data from various regulated and non-regulated sources to create a single, comprehensive customer profile.
*   **Enhance Digital Engagement:** Significantly improve the functionality, usability, and personalization of Quantum Energy's digital self-service channels.
*   **Empower Customer Service:** Provide customer service representatives with advanced tools and training to deliver efficient and highly satisfying support.
*   **Enable Personalized Communications:** Deliver targeted, relevant, and proactive communications to customers based on their needs, preferences, and product portfolio.
*   **Streamline Customer Journeys:** Optimize key customer journeys (e.g., onboarding, billing inquiry, outage management, non-regulated product support) for efficiency and satisfaction.

## 2. Key Program Components & Deliverables

The Unified Customer Experience Program is structured into several interconnected projects:

### 2.1. Integrated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Platform (Project A)

*   **Description:** Implementation and integration of a new enterprise-wide CRM platform that serves as the central hub for all customer interactions and data, for both regulated and non-regulated activities. This will provide a 360-degree view of the customer.
*   **Key Deliverables:** Unified CRM system across regulated Customer Service and AES Sales/Marketing by 2027, consolidated customer interaction history, single view of customer accounts and product subscriptions.
*   **Enabling Capabilities:** 10.1 Customer Contact & Interaction Management, 10.2 Customer Account Management, 10.8 Customer Insights & Analytics.

### 2.2. Enhanced Digital Self-Service Channels (Project B)

*   **Description:** Overhaul and enhancement of Quantum Energy's web portal and mobile application to provide robust self-service capabilities. This includes intuitive interfaces for billing, usage data, outage reporting (regulated), and managing non-regulated smart home/EV products.
*   **Key Deliverables:** Redesigned web portal (launch 2026), updated mobile app with new features (launch 2026/2027), AI-powered chatbot for common inquiries by 2028.
*   **Enabling Capabilities:** 10.5 Digital Customer Experience, 10.3 Service Request & Fulfillment.

### 2.3. Proactive & Personalized Communications (Project C)

*   **Description:** Development and implementation of an advanced communication platform capable of delivering targeted, proactive, and personalized messages to customers. This includes multi-channel notifications for regulated service alerts (e.g., outages, high usage) and non-regulated product updates or offers.
*   **Key Deliverables:** Multi-channel notification engine by 2027, customer communication preference center, personalized messaging templates, enhanced outage communication system.
*   **Enabling Capabilities:** 10.6 Customer Communications & Notifications, 10.8 Customer Insights & Analytics.

### 2.4. Customer Service Transformation (Project D)

*   **Description:** Modernization of contact center operations, including new agent desktop tools, improved knowledge management systems, and comprehensive training programs designed to support an integrated customer experience across regulated and non-regulated offerings.
*   **Key Deliverables:** New agent desktop by 2028, unified knowledge base, cross-functional training modules, enhanced customer service quality metrics.
*   **Enabling Capabilities:** 10.4 Customer Inquiry & Complaint Resolution, 15.3 Learning & Development.

## 3. Benefits & Value Realization

*   **Increased Customer Satisfaction:** Achieves NPS target of 50+ through seamless and personalized interactions.
*   **Reduced Operational Costs:** Drives digital self-service adoption, reducing call center volume and improving agent efficiency.
*   **Enhanced Non-Regulated Growth:** Supports personalized offers and marketing for AES products, contributing to revenue objectives.
*   **Improved Brand Loyalty:** Fosters a unified and positive brand perception across all Quantum Energy offerings.
*   **Data-Driven Decisions:** Provides a robust platform for customer insights, informing product development and service improvements.

---

## 4. Key Dependencies

*   **CDP-001 (Customer Data Platform Implementation):** UCE-001 is highly dependent on the CDP for foundational customer data and analytics capabilities. The CDP must be operational to feed the CRM and communication platforms.
*   **DGPR-001 (Digital Grid Program):** Real-time AMI and outage data from the Digital Grid Program will be crucial for enhancing proactive communications and digital self-service.
*   **AES-G1 (Advanced Energy Solutions Growth Initiative):** Success of non-regulated product launches relies on the improved digital engagement and service capabilities provided by UCE-001.
*   **WFD-001 (Workforce Digitalization & Enablement):** New tools and training from UCE-001 will contribute to the broader workforce digitalization efforts.
