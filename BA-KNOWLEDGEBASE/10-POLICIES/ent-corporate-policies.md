## 1. Introduction

Corporate policies are established by Quantum Energy's leadership to define acceptable behavior, manage risks, and ensure the consistent application of principles across the organization. They complement external regulations and provide a framework for day-to-day decision-making and operations.

## 2. Key Enterprise Corporate Policies

### 2.1. Code of Business Conduct and Ethics

*   **Policy Statement:** Quantum Energy is committed to conducting its business with the highest standards of integrity, ethics, and compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.
*   **Purpose:** To guide all employees, officers, and directors in making ethical decisions and acting with integrity in all business dealings.
*   **Key Provisions:** Anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, protection of company assets, confidentiality, non-discrimination, reporting violations.
*   **Impact:** Foundational policy for all Quantum Energy employees across all business units. Critical for maintaining public trust and investor confidence.

### 2.2. Data Privacy and Protection Policy

*   **Policy Statement:** Quantum Energy is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data entrusted to it by customers, employees, and other stakeholders, adhering to all applicable data privacy laws and industry best practices.
*   **Purpose:** To define standards for the collection, use, storage, processing, transfer, and retention of personal data.
*   **Key Provisions:** Data minimization, consent requirements (especially for non-regulated data sharing), individual rights (access, rectification, erasure), data breach response, third-party data sharing agreements.
*   **Impact:** Applies to all data collected and processed by Quantum Energy. Especially critical for non-regulated products involving granular customer data (e.g., smart home telemetry, EV charging data), ensuring compliance with laws like CDPA.

### 2.3. Cybersecurity Policy

*   **Policy Statement:** Quantum Energy is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of its information systems, operational technology (OT) systems, and data assets from cyber threats.
*   **Purpose:** To establish a comprehensive framework for managing cybersecurity risks across IT and OT environments.
*   **Key Provisions:** Access control, network security, incident response, vulnerability management, security awareness training, NERC CIP compliance (for regulated assets), supply chain security.
*   **Impact:** Applies to all Quantum Energy systems, including regulated grid control systems and non-regulated product platforms. Crucial for operational resilience and customer trust.

### 2.4. Environmental, Health & Safety (EH&S) Policy

*   **Policy Statement:** Quantum Energy is committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace for its employees, protecting the public, and minimizing its environmental impact in all operations.
*   **Purpose:** To define Quantum Energy's commitment to EH&S excellence and establish fundamental principles for managing EH&S risks.
*   **Key Provisions:** Risk assessment, incident reporting and investigation, training, emergency preparedness, waste management, emissions control, contractor safety.
*   **Impact:** Applies to all operational activities, from regulated power generation and field work to non-regulated product installations.

### 2.5. Procurement and Supply Chain Policy

*   **Policy Statement:** Quantum Energy is committed to fair, ethical, and transparent procurement practices, ensuring the acquisition of goods and services that provide best value while upholding our ethical and sustainability standards.
*   **Purpose:** To govern the sourcing, selection, and management of suppliers and contractors.
*   **Key Provisions:** Supplier diversity, competitive bidding, contract management, anti-bribery, quality assurance, ethical sourcing.
*   **Impact:** Applies to all procurement activities, including regulated infrastructure components and non-regulated product hardware/software.

### 2.6. Investment Governance Policy

*   **Policy Statement:** Quantum Energy maintains a disciplined process for evaluating, approving, and monitoring capital and strategic investments to ensure optimal allocation of resources and achievement of strategic objectives.
*   **Purpose:** To establish a consistent framework for investment decision-making.
*   **Key Provisions:** Business case requirements, financial justification, risk assessment, approval authorities, post-implementation review (PIR).
*   **Impact:** Applies to all major capital projects (regulated infrastructure, IT systems) and strategic initiatives (non-regulated product development, market expansion). Ensures balanced investment across regulated and non-regulated portfolios.

## 3. Policy Management

*   **Ownership:** Each corporate policy is assigned a senior executive owner responsible for its content and adherence.
*   **Review Cycle:** Policies are reviewed and updated on a regular schedule (typically annually or biennially) or as triggered by significant internal or external changes.
*   **Accessibility:** All policies are made readily available to employees via the Quantum Energy intranet.
*   **Training:** Mandatory training is provided for key policies (e.g., Code of Conduct, Cybersecurity) to all relevant employees.
