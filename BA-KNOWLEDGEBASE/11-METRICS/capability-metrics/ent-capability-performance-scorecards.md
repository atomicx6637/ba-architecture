## Executive Summary

This document defines performance scorecards for a selection of Quantum Energy's critical business capabilities. These scorecards provide a focused view of each capability's operational effectiveness, efficiency, and contribution to strategic objectives. By tracking these metrics, we can identify areas for improvement, validate the impact of initiatives, and ensure that our capabilities are performing optimally across both regulated and non-regulated operations.

---

## 1. Introduction

Capability performance metrics delve deeper than strategic metrics, measuring the "health" and execution quality of individual business capabilities. Each scorecard is designed to provide a balanced view, encompassing various aspects of capability performance.

## 3. Reporting and Analysis

*   **Frequency:** Capability scorecards will be reviewed monthly by relevant department heads and quarterly by the executive owner.
*   **Actionable Insights:** Performance data will be analyzed to identify root causes of underperformance and inform corrective actions or new initiatives.
*   **Integration:** These scorecards feed into the Enterprise Balanced Scorecard and directly support the tracking of strategic objectives.
