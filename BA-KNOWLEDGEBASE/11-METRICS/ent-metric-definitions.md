## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive glossary of key metrics used across Quantum Energy's various performance measurement frameworks, including the Balanced Scorecard, strategic metrics, capability metrics, value stream KPIs, and benefit realization metrics. The purpose of this document is to ensure a common understanding, consistent calculation, and accurate interpretation of our performance measures across all business units—both regulated and non-regulated—thereby supporting data-driven decision-making and accountability.

---

## 1. Introduction

Consistent metric definitions are fundamental for effective performance management. This glossary serves as the authoritative source for understanding "what" each metric measures, "how" it is calculated, and "why" it is important to Quantum Energy.

---

## 2. General Metric Attributes

Each metric definition will typically include:
*   **Metric Name:** Unique identifier.
*   **Definition:** Clear and concise explanation of what the metric measures.
*   **Formula/Calculation:** The mathematical formula used to derive the metric.
*   **Unit of Measure:** The standard unit (e.g., %, Count, $, Score, Days).
*   **Purpose/Rationale:** Why the metric is important and what insight it provides.
*   **Data Source(s):** Where the raw data for calculation originates.
*   **Frequency:** How often the metric is calculated and reported.
*   **Owner:** The business function or role accountable for the metric's accuracy and integrity.
*   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** Linkage to Quantum Energy's high-level goals.

## 3. Key Metric Definitions

### 3.1. Customer-Centric Metrics

*   **Enterprise Net Promoter Score (NPS)**
    *   **Definition:** A customer loyalty metric that measures the willingness of customers to recommend Quantum Energy's products or services to others.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Percentage of Promoters) - (Percentage of Detractors). Promoters score 9-10; Passives 7-8; Detractors 0-6 on a 0-10 likelihood to recommend scale.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** Score (typically -100 to +100).
    *   **Purpose:** Overall customer satisfaction and loyalty across regulated and non-regulated offerings.
    *   **Data Source(s):** Customer surveys.
    *   **Frequency:** Quarterly.
    *   **Owner:** Chief Customer Officer.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 2.1. Unified Customer Satisfaction.
*   **Digital Self-Service Adoption Rate**
    *   **Definition:** The percentage of eligible customer transactions that are completed through Quantum Energy's digital channels (web portal, mobile app) without requiring agent assistance.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Number of Digital Self-Service Transactions / Total Number of Eligible Transactions) * 100%.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** %.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures efficiency of digital channels and customer preference for self-service.
    *   **Data Source(s):** Web analytics, mobile app analytics, CRM/CIS transaction logs.
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Customer Service & Experience.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 2.2. Digital Self-Service Adoption.
*   **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)**
    *   **Definition:** A metric that measures how satisfied customers are with a specific interaction or experience (e.g., post-installation, post-service call).
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Number of Satisfied Customers / Total Number of Respondents) * 100%. (Satisfied customers typically score 4-5 on a 1-5 scale).
    *   **Unit of Measure:** %.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures immediate customer satisfaction with specific touchpoints.
    *   **Data Source(s):** Post-interaction surveys.
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly (event-driven).
    *   **Owner:** VP, Customer Service & Experience / VP, Operations & Delivery - AES.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 2.1. Unified Customer Satisfaction.
*   **First Contact Resolution (FCR) Rate**
    *   **Definition:** The percentage of customer inquiries or issues resolved completely on the first contact, without requiring follow-up.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Number of Inquiries Resolved on First Contact / Total Number of Inquiries) * 100%.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** %.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures contact center efficiency and customer experience.
    *   **Data Source(s):** Call center/CRM logs.
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Customer Service & Experience.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 2.1. Unified Customer Satisfaction.

### 3.2. Regulated Operational Metrics

*   **System Average Interruption Duration Index (SAIDI)**
    *   **Definition:** The average duration of sustained power interruptions for the average customer served by Quantum Energy's electric distribution system over a given period.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** Sum of all customer interruption durations / Total number of customers served.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** Minutes.
    *   **Purpose:** Key measure of electric service reliability (how long customers are out of power).
    *   **Data Source(s):** Outage Management System (OMS).
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly/Annually.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Electric Distribution.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 1.1. Electric Grid Reliability Enhancement.
*   **System Average Interruption Frequency Index (SAIFI)**
    *   **Definition:** The average number of sustained power interruptions that a customer served by Quantum Energy's electric distribution system experiences over a given period.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** Total number of customer interruptions / Total number of customers served.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** Count.
    *   **Purpose:** Key measure of electric service reliability (how often customers are out of power).
    *   **Data Source(s):** Outage Management System (OMS).
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly/Annually.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Electric Distribution.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 1.1. Electric Grid Reliability Enhancement.
*   **Preventable Gas Incident Rate**
    *   **Definition:** The number of natural gas incidents (e.g., leaks, damage) caused by Quantum Energy's operations or deemed preventable, per 100 miles of main.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Number of Preventable Incidents / Total Miles of Gas Main) * 100.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** Per 100 miles of main.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures gas pipeline safety performance and regulatory compliance.
    *   **Data Source(s):** Gas Safety Database, Incident Reporting System.
    *   **Frequency:** Quarterly.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Natural Gas Distribution.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 1.2. Gas Infrastructure Safety & Integrity.

### 3.3. Non-Regulated Business Metrics

*   **Non-Regulated Revenue as % of Total Company Revenue**
    *   **Definition:** The percentage of Quantum Energy's total revenue derived from its non-regulated advanced energy solutions business unit.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Total Non-Regulated Revenue / Total Company Revenue) * 100%.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** %.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures strategic diversification and growth of the non-regulated portfolio.
    *   **Data Source(s):** Enterprise Financial Systems.
    *   **Frequency:** Quarterly/Annually.
    *   **Owner:** CDIO / CFO.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 5.1. Non-Regulated Revenue Contribution.
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
    *   **Definition:** The total cost spent on sales and marketing to acquire one new paying customer for a non-regulated product or service.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** Total Sales & Marketing Spend / Number of New Customers Acquired.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** $.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures the efficiency of customer acquisition efforts in competitive non-regulated markets.
    *   **Data Source(s):** Sales & Marketing systems, Financial Systems.
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Sales & Marketing - AES.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 5.1. Non-Regulated Revenue Contribution.
*   **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Growth**
    *   **Definition:** The month-over-month growth rate of predictable and recurring revenue generated from subscription-based non-regulated products.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** ((MRR Current Month - MRR Previous Month) / MRR Previous Month) * 100%.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** %.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures the growth trajectory and health of subscription-based non-regulated offerings.
    *   **Data Source(s):** AES Billing Systems, Financial Systems.
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Product Development - AES / VP, Sales & Marketing - AES.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 5.1. Non-Regulated Revenue Contribution.
*   **Customer Churn Rate (Non-Regulated)**
    *   **Definition:** The rate at which customers discontinue their subscription to a non-regulated product or service over a given period.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Number of Customers Lost / Total Number of Customers at Start of Period) * 100%.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** %.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures customer retention and satisfaction with non-regulated offerings.
    *   **Data Source(s):** AES Customer Management Systems.
    *   **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly.
    *   **Owner:** VP, Product Development - AES / VP, Sales & Marketing - AES.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 2.1. Unified Customer Satisfaction.

### 3.4. Learning & Growth Metrics

*   **Employee Engagement Score**
    *   **Definition:** A measure of employees' emotional commitment to Quantum Energy and its goals, reflecting their enthusiasm for work and dedication.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** Based on results from annual employee engagement surveys (composite score).
    *   **Unit of Measure:** Score (1-5 or 1-100).
    *   **Purpose:** Measures organizational health, talent retention, and cultural alignment.
    *   **Data Source(s):** HR Employee Engagement Surveys.
    *   **Frequency:** Annually.
    *   **Owner:** CHRO.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 4.4. Workforce Digitalization (indirect).
*   **R&D Investment Rate (Non-Regulated)**
    *   **Definition:** The percentage of non-regulated revenue reinvested into research and development for new energy solutions.
    *   **Formula/Calculation:** (Total AES R&D Spend / Total AES Revenue) * 100%.
    *   **Unit of Measure:** %.
    *   **Purpose:** Measures commitment to innovation and future non-regulated product pipeline.
    *   **Data Source(s):** AES Financial Systems.
    *   **Frequency:** Annually.
    *   **Owner:** CDIO / CFO.
    *   **Related Strategic Objective(s):** 3.4. New Energy Solutions R&D Investment.
