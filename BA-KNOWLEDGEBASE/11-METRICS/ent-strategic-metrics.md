## Executive Summary

This document defines the key enterprise-level metrics that directly measure Quantum Energy's progress against its 2025-2030 strategic objectives. These metrics are crucial for monitoring the execution of our strategy, identifying areas of success, and highlighting where further focus or intervention is required. They encompass both regulated operational excellence and non-regulated growth drivers, providing a holistic view of our performance.

---

## 1. Introduction

Strategic metrics are high-level, outcome-oriented measures that directly align with Quantum Energy's strategic objectives. They are used by executive leadership and the Board of Directors to track overall company performance and provide input to the Enterprise Balanced Scorecard.

## 3. Reporting and Analysis

*   **Executive Dashboard:** Strategic metrics are visualized on an executive dashboard, updated monthly or quarterly.
*   **Performance Reviews:** Quarterly performance reviews are conducted by the ELT and Board of Directors, assessing progress against these targets.
*   **Root Cause Analysis:** Deviations from targets trigger root cause analysis and potential adjustments to strategic initiatives or operational plans.
