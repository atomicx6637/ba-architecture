## Executive Summary

This document defines key performance indicators (KPIs) for a selection of Quantum Energy's non-regulated value streams. These KPIs measure the market performance, customer adoption, and profitability of our advanced energy solutions, directly reflecting our ability to innovate, compete, and grow in dynamic markets. Tracking these metrics ensures accountability, drives product improvements, and supports our non-regulated growth strategy.

---

## 1. Introduction

Value stream KPIs for non-regulated offerings focus on market-driven outcomes, customer value perception, and financial viability. They provide insights into the effectiveness of our product development, sales, and delivery processes in competitive environments.

## 3. Reporting and Analysis

*   **Frequency:** KPIs will be monitored monthly or quarterly by value stream owners and executive sponsors within the AES business unit.
*   **Dashboards:** Performance dashboards will be maintained, visualizing current performance against aggressive growth targets.
*   **Product Reviews:** KPIs are critical inputs for regular product reviews, informing decisions on feature enhancements, pricing, and marketing strategies.
*   **Market Benchmarking:** Performance will be regularly benchmarked against competitive offerings in the non-regulated market.
