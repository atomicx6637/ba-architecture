## Executive Summary

This document defines key performance indicators (KPIs) for a selection of Quantum Energy's regulated value streams. These KPIs measure the efficiency, effectiveness, and compliance of our core utility operations, directly reflecting our ability to deliver reliable, safe, and affordable electric and natural gas service to our customers. Tracking these metrics ensures accountability, drives operational improvements, and supports regulatory reporting.

---

## 1. Introduction

Value stream KPIs provide a holistic view of the performance of end-to-end processes, focusing on outcomes and stakeholder value. For regulated value streams, these KPIs are often subject to regulatory scrutiny and directly impact our reputation and financial recovery.

## 3. Reporting and Analysis

*   **Frequency:** KPIs will be monitored monthly or quarterly by value stream owners and executive sponsors.
*   **Dashboards:** Performance dashboards will visualize current performance against targets, highlighting trends and deviations.
*   **Performance Reviews:** Regular performance reviews will be conducted to identify root causes of underperformance and drive corrective actions or process improvements.
*   **Regulatory Reporting:** Many of these KPIs are directly used in regulatory filings and reports.
