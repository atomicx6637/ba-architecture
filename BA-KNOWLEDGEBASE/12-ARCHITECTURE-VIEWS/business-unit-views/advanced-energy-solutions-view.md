## Executive Summary

This document provides a high-level architectural view of Quantum Energy's Advanced Energy Solutions (AES) Business Unit. It highlights the core capabilities, value streams, products, and information flows essential for the rapid development, effective marketing, and scalable delivery of non-regulated advanced energy solutions. This view is critical for AES leadership, product teams, and partners to understand the structure and functioning of our non-regulated growth engine.

---

## 1. Introduction

The AES Business Unit is Quantum Energy's strategic vehicle for innovation and diversification, operating in competitive energy solutions markets. This view distills the agile, customer-centric, and technology-driven nature of AES into a clear architectural representation.

## 4. Key Non-Regulated Value Streams

The following value streams represent the end-to-end processes for delivering non-regulated products and services:

*   **NCV-01: Smart Home Solution Delivery:** Marketing, selling, installing, and supporting smart home energy management solutions.
*   **NCV-02: EV Charging Solution Deployment:** Marketing, selling, deploying, and supporting EV charging infrastructure.
*   **NCV-03: Distributed Generation (DG) & Storage Solution Provision:** Designing, proposing, installing, and maintaining DG and storage solutions.
*   **NCV-04: Non-Regulated Energy Advisory & Consulting:** Providing expert advice and analytics for energy optimization.
*   **SMV-03: Distributed Energy Resource (DER) Aggregation & Optimization:** Aggregating and optimizing DERs for grid services (often from AES-delivered assets).
*   **SMV-04: Non-Regulated Energy Market Participation:** Engaging in competitive energy markets with non-regulated assets.

## 5. Critical Information & Data Flows

Information is central to AES's operations, driving product intelligence and customer personalization. Key information entities and flows include:

*   **Non-Regulated Customer Profile:** Detailed customer data including product subscriptions, preferences, and engagement history for AES offerings.
*   **Smart Device Data:** Granular telemetry, status, and control logs from customer-sited smart devices, EV chargers, and DERs.
*   **Product Definition Data:** Features, pricing, and terms for all non-regulated products.
*   **Market Intelligence Data:** Competitive landscape, market trends, customer demands for non-regulated sectors.
*   **Key Data Flows:**
    *   **Smart Home Telemetry & Control Flow:** (Bidirectional data between devices and platform).
    *   **EV Charging Data & Management Flow:** (Charging session data, platform management).
    *   **Non-Regulated Product Sales & Onboarding Flow:** (Lead to customer acquisition to activation).
    *   **DER Aggregation & Grid Services Flow:** (Data from customer-sited DERs to DERMS).

*(Refer to `ent-business-information-model.md` and `non-regulated-data-flows.md` for more details)*

## 6. Organizational & Regulatory Context

*   **Organizational Structure:** Managed by the Advanced Energy Solutions (AES) Business Unit under the Chief Digital & Innovation Officer (CDIO), fostering agility and market responsiveness.
*   **Interfaces with Regulated Operations:** AES collaborates closely with Regulated Operations for DER interconnection, grid impact analysis, and leveraging utility infrastructure where permissible. Strict protocols for data sharing and cost allocation are followed.
*   **Shared Services:** Leverages Enterprise Services for IT, HR, Finance, Legal, and Procurement.
*   **Regulatory Context:** Operates in competitive markets, subject to general consumer protection laws, data privacy regulations (e.g., CDPA), and market conduct rules, but largely outside traditional utility rate regulation.
