## Executive Summary

This document provides a detailed Level 3 (L3) decomposition for selected core Customer Engagement & Service capabilities within Quantum Energy. This granular view helps in understanding the operational functions that deliver our integrated customer experience, spanning both regulated utility services and non-regulated advanced offerings. This detail is crucial for designing seamless customer journeys, optimizing digital channels, and personalizing interactions.

This L3 decomposition extends the Level 1 and Level 2 capabilities defined in `ent-capabilities-l1-l2.md`. Each L3 capability further breaks down a Level 2 capability into more specific, operational functions.

## 2. Customer Engagement & Service L3 Capabilities

**Parent L1 Capability:** 10. Customer Engagement & Service (Ent)
**Definition:** The enterprise-wide capability to interact with customers across all touchpoints, deliver service experiences for both regulated and non-regulated offerings, and manage ongoing customer relationships.

### 2.1. Parent L2 Capability: 10.5. Digital Customer Experience

**Definition:** The capability to design and deliver seamless digital interactions through web, mobile, and portal platforms for both regulated self-service and non-regulated product management.

#### L3 Capabilities:

*   **10.5.1. Customer Web Portal Management:** Develop, maintain, and enhance the online customer portal, including secure login, account summary, billing history, and usage data visualization.
*   **10.5.2. Mobile Application Development & Management:** Design, build, and maintain native mobile applications (iOS/Android) offering core self-service features, outage reporting, and non-regulated product control.
*   **10.5.3. Online Self-Service Transaction Processing:** Enable customers to complete common transactions digitally, such as starting/stopping service, making payments, enrolling in programs, and managing non-regulated subscriptions.
*   **10.5.4. Digital Communication Channel Management:** Integrate and manage various digital communication channels (e.g., email, SMS, push notifications, chat) for customer support and proactive messaging.
*   **10.5.5. Personalization & Content Delivery:** Deliver tailored content, offers, and insights to customers via digital channels based on their preferences, usage patterns, and regulated/non-regulated product ownership.
*   **10.5.6. Digital Accessibility Compliance:** Ensure all digital platforms meet accessibility standards (e.g., WCAG) to provide an inclusive experience for all customers.

### 2.2. Parent L2 Capability: 10.8. Customer Insights & Analytics

**Definition:** The capability to analyze customer data and behavior (from both regulated and non-regulated interactions) to develop insights that inform strategy and personalization.

#### L3 Capabilities:

*   **10.8.1. Customer Data Collection & Integration:** Gather and integrate customer data from diverse sources including billing systems, AMI, CRM, web analytics, social media, and non-regulated product usage data.
*   **10.8.2. Customer Segmentation & Profiling:** Develop and maintain granular customer segments and detailed profiles based on demographics, consumption patterns, preferences, and non-regulated product adoption.
*   **10.8.3. Behavioral Analytics & Modeling:** Analyze customer interactions and usage data to understand behaviors, predict future actions (e.g., churn, program enrollment), and identify cross-sell opportunities.
*   **10.8.4. Voice of Customer (VoC) Program Management:** Design, implement, and analyze feedback mechanisms (surveys, call recordings, social listening) to capture customer sentiment and identify pain points.
*   **10.8.5. Personalized Offer & Recommendation Engine:** Develop and manage systems that generate personalized product recommendations and energy-saving tips for individual customers (regulated and non-regulated).
*   **10.8.6. Customer Journey Mapping & Optimization:** Conduct end-to-end mapping of customer journeys, identify critical touchpoints, and leverage insights to continuously improve the customer experience.
