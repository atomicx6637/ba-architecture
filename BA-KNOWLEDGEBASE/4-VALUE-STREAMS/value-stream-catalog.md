## 2. Value Stream Categories (Overview)

Quantum Energy's value streams are organized into six categories to provide a clear structure for understanding how value is delivered across the enterprise:

1.  **Regulated Customer Value Streams:** Focus on the delivery of core electric and natural gas utility services.
2.  **Non-Regulated Customer Value Streams:** Focus on the delivery of advanced energy solutions and services (e.g., smart home, EV charging, DG).
3.  **Asset and Operations Value Streams:** Focus on infrastructure and operational execution for both regulated and non-regulated assets.
4.  **Supply and Market Value Streams:** Focus on energy commodity procurement and market participation.
5.  **Regulatory & External Affairs Value Streams:** Focus on regulatory obligations, policy engagement, and stakeholder relations.
6.  **Enterprise Support Value Streams:** Focus on internal enablement and corporate functions supporting all business segments.
