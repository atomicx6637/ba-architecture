## Executive Summary

This document details the stages of the Enterprise Strategic Planning & Execution value stream (ESV-01). This is the primary leadership value stream that describes how Quantum Energy sets its overall direction, allocates resources to achieve its goals, and monitors progress. It is the "brain" of the organization, translating board and executive intent into tangible action and results across all business units.

---

## 1. Value Stream Context

**Value Stream ID:** ESV-01
**Name:** Enterprise Strategic Planning & Execution
**Description:** Develop and execute Quantum Energy's overall enterprise strategy, ensuring alignment, resource allocation, and performance management across all regulated and non-regulated businesses.

**Entry Criteria:**
-   The annual or multi-year strategic planning cycle is formally initiated.
-   Analysis of the external market, competitive landscape, and internal performance is available.
-   Input from the Board of Directors and key stakeholders has been collected.

**Exit Criteria:**
-   The enterprise strategy is formally defined, approved, and communicated.
-   Financial and human resources are allocated to the highest-priority strategic initiatives.
-   A performance management system is actively tracking progress against strategic goals.
-   A regular cadence of performance reporting and review is established.

## 3. Cross-Value Stream Dependencies

*   **ESV-01** provides the direction and funding for every other value stream in the enterprise. It sets the objectives that other value streams must execute against.
*   It is a primary consumer of information from all other value streams to inform its "Environmental Scanning" and "Performance Monitoring" stages.
*   It has a particularly strong link with **ESV-03 Financial Management & Reporting**, as the budget is the most direct expression of strategy.
*   It also has a strong link with **ESV-02 Talent Management** to ensure the workforce plan aligns with strategic needs.
