## Executive Summary

This document details the stages of the Information Technology & Digital Service Delivery value stream (ESV-04). This value stream describes how Quantum Energy plans, builds, runs, and supports all technology and digital solutions for the enterprise. It covers everything from developing a new mobile app, to keeping the email servers running, to protecting the company from cyber-attacks.

---

## 1. Value Stream Context

**Value Stream ID:** ESV-04
**Name:** Information Technology & Digital Service Delivery
**Description:** Provide secure, reliable, and innovative technology infrastructure, applications, and digital services that enable all Quantum Energy's business operations and strategic initiatives.

**Entry Criteria:**
-   A business need for a new or enhanced technology solution is identified.
-   A technology or cybersecurity incident occurs.
-   An employee or customer initiates a request for IT support or service.

**Exit Criteria:**
-   A new technology solution is deployed and adopted by the business.
-   IT services are delivered within agreed-upon service levels (SLAs).
-   The company's cybersecurity posture is maintained, and incidents are resolved.
-   User satisfaction with IT services meets or exceeds targets.

## 3. Cross-Value Stream Dependencies

*   **ESV-04** is a foundational enabling value stream for all others, as modern business processes are heavily reliant on technology.
*   It works closely with **ESV-01** to ensure the IT strategy and portfolio align with the overall enterprise strategy.
*   The budget for IT is a significant component of the process managed by **ESV-03 Financial Management & Reporting**.
*   It is a key partner to **ESV-05 Enterprise Risk Management**, as cybersecurity is one of the company's top risks.
*   It builds and runs the platforms that enable digital-native value streams like **SMV-03 DER Aggregation & Optimization**.
