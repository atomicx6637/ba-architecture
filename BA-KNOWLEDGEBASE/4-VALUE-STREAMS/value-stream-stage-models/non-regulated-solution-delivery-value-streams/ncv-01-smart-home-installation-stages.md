## Executive Summary

This document details the stages of the Smart Home Solution Delivery value stream (NCV-01) for Quantum Energy's non-regulated Advanced Energy Solutions (AES) business unit. This value stream outlines the end-to-end process of marketing, selling, installing, and activating smart home energy management solutions. Understanding these stages is critical for optimizing customer experience, ensuring efficient delivery, and driving profitability for our non-regulated offerings.

---

## 1. Value Stream Context

**Value Stream ID:** NCV-01
**Name:** Smart Home Solution Delivery
**Description:** The process by which Quantum Energy's non-regulated AES unit acquires customers for smart home solutions, designs a tailored package, installs the necessary devices, activates the platform, and provides initial customer support.

**Entry Criteria:**
-   Customer expresses interest in Quantum Energy's smart home solutions.
-   Customer contact information is available.
-   Service is available in the customer's geographic area.

**Exit Criteria:**
-   Smart home solution is fully installed and activated.
-   Customer is onboarded and actively using the smart home platform.
-   Solution is performing as expected.
-   Customer satisfaction confirmed for the non-regulated product.

## 3. Cross-Value Stream Dependencies

*   **NCV-01 Smart Home Solution Delivery** is dependent on **11. Non-Regulated Product Development** for the solutions being offered.
*   It leverages **RCV-02 Regulated Customer Service Delivery** for any utility-related issues or inquiries from customers.
*   It relies on **AOV-03 Work & Outage Management** for efficient field installation and maintenance.
*   Data collected during this value stream (e.g., energy consumption data from smart thermostats) feeds into **10.8 Customer Insights & Analytics**.
*   It can be influenced by **SMV-03 Distributed Energy Resource (DER) Aggregation & Optimization** if smart home devices contribute to grid services.
