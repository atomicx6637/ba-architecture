## Executive Summary

This document details the stages of the Non-Regulated Energy Advisory & Consulting value stream (NCV-04). This professional service offering focuses on providing expertise to large commercial and industrial (C&I) customers to help them manage energy costs, optimize consumption, and achieve sustainability goals. This value stream positions Quantum Energy as a strategic partner to its largest customers, moving beyond commodity service to value-added solutions.

---

## 1. Value Stream Context

**Value Stream ID:** NCV-04
**Name:** Non-Regulated Energy Advisory & Consulting
**Description:** Provide expert advice, analytics, and consulting services to commercial and industrial customers to optimize energy use, manage costs, and achieve sustainability goals.

**Entry Criteria:**
-   A C&I customer identifies a need for energy strategy, cost optimization, or sustainability consulting.
-   The scope of the consulting engagement is defined.
-   A master service agreement or statement of work is executed.

**Exit Criteria:**
-   The advisory or consulting engagement is completed as per the statement of work.
-   Final recommendations and deliverables are presented to the customer.
-   The customer realizes or has a clear path to realizing the projected benefits.
-   Customer satisfaction with the non-regulated service is confirmed.

## 3. Cross-Value Stream Dependencies

*   This value stream often serves as a "front-end" for others, identifying opportunities that lead to **NCV-02 EV Charging Solution Deployment** or **NCV-03 DG & Storage Solution Provision** projects.
*   Insights from these engagements provide valuable input to **ESV-01 Enterprise Strategic Planning & Execution** regarding market trends and customer needs.
*   It requires access to detailed energy data, which is collected as part of the regulated **Metering and Measurement** capability, requiring a clear data-sharing agreement.
*   Success in this value stream enhances the overall corporate relationship, which is also managed by **REV-03 External Stakeholder Engagement**.
