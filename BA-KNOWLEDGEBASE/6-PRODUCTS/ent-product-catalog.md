## Executive Summary

This document defines the comprehensive product and service catalog for Quantum Energy, a vertically integrated natural gas and electric utility also offering advanced energy solutions. Products are organized by category and clearly distinguish between regulated utility services and non-regulated offerings. This catalog provides a holistic view of "what we sell" to all customer segments, highlighting value propositions, target markets, and enabling capabilities.

In BIZBOK terminology, **products** are tangible or intangible goods and services that the organization offers to stakeholders (primarily customers) to fulfill value propositions. This catalog maps to:
-   **Capabilities:** Products are delivered through business capabilities.
-   **Value Streams:** Products are the outcomes of value stream execution.
-   **Stakeholders:** Products serve specific customer segments and stakeholder needs.
-   **Information:** Products generate transaction and usage data.

## Product Catalog Structure

The product catalog is organized into seven primary categories, with clear distinctions between regulated and non-regulated offerings where applicable:

1.  **Core Regulated Electric Energy Products (Reg)**
2.  **Core Regulated Natural Gas Energy Products (Reg)**
3.  **Regulated Dual-Fuel Products (Reg)**
4.  **Non-Regulated Smart Home & Digital Solutions (NonReg)**
5.  **Non-Regulated EV & Transportation Solutions (NonReg)**
6.  **Non-Regulated Distributed Energy & Resiliency Solutions (NonReg)**
7.  **Enterprise Advisory & Utility-Supporting Services (Ent / Mixed)**

Each product entry includes:
-   Product name and identifier
-   Description and value proposition
-   Regulatory/Non-Regulated Scope
-   Target customer segments
-   Delivery model
-   Key enabling capabilities

## 1. Core Regulated Electric Energy Products (Reg)

These products represent the foundational electric services provided under regulatory oversight.

### 1.1. Residential Electric Service

**Product ID:** ELEC-RES-001
**Scope:** Regulated
**Description:** Standard electric energy delivery service for residential customers including single-family homes, apartments, and condominiums.
**Value Proposition:** Reliable, safe electric power to support household lighting, appliances, heating/cooling, and electronics.

**Delivery Model:** Continuous service with monthly billing.
**Key Enabling L1 Capabilities:** Electric Distribution, Metering and Measurement, Financial Management (Billing), Customer Engagement & Service.

### 1.2. Commercial Electric Service (Small)

**Product ID:** ELEC-COM-001
**Scope:** Regulated
**Description:** Electric energy delivery for small commercial customers (e.g., retail, office, service businesses) with demand typically under 50 kW.
**Value Proposition:** Dependable electric service to support business operations, equipment, lighting, and customer comfort.
**Customer Segments:** Regulated Commercial Customers (Small).
**Delivery Model:** Continuous service with monthly billing.
**Key Enabling L1 Capabilities:** Electric Distribution, Metering and Measurement, Financial Management (Billing), Customer Engagement & Service.

### 1.3. Commercial Electric Service (Large)

**Product ID:** ELEC-COM-002
**Scope:** Regulated
**Description:** Electric energy delivery for larger commercial customers with demand typically between 50 kW and 1 MW.
**Value Proposition:** Scalable, reliable electric service with rate options and account support tailored to business needs.
**Customer Segments:** Regulated Commercial Customers (Large).
**Delivery Model:** Continuous service with monthly billing and dedicated account support.
**Key Enabling L1 Capabilities:** Electric Distribution, Energy Transmission, Metering and Measurement, Financial Management (Billing), Customer Engagement & Service.

### 1.4. Industrial Electric Service

**Product ID:** ELEC-IND-001
**Scope:** Regulated

**Customer Segments:** Regulated Industrial Customers.
**Delivery Model:** Continuous service with customized billing and energy management.
**Key Enabling L1 Capabilities:** Energy Transmission, Electric Distribution, Energy Generation, Metering and Measurement, Financial Management (Billing), Customer Engagement & Service.

**Product ID:** ELEC-MUN-001

## 6. Non-Regulated Distributed Energy & Resiliency Solutions (NonReg)

These products focus on local energy generation, storage, and grid interaction.

### 6.1. Residential Solar + Storage Solution

**Product ID:** NONREG-DG-001
**Scope:** Non-Regulated
**Description:** Design, installation, and integration of rooftop solar PV systems combined with home battery storage for residential customers.
**Value Proposition:** Energy independence, bill savings, backup power during outages, and reduced carbon footprint.
**Customer Segments:** Non-Regulated DG & Storage Adopters (Residential).
**Delivery Model:** System purchase, financing options, professional installation, optional O&M.
**Key Enabling L1 Capabilities:** Non-Regulated Product Development, Non-Regulated Sales & Marketing, Procurement & Supply Chain Management, Electric Distribution (Interconnection).

### 6.2. Commercial Microgrid-as-a-Service

**Product ID:** NONREG-DG-002
**Scope:** Non-Regulated
**Description:** Development, installation, and ongoing operation of localized microgrids for commercial and industrial clients, providing enhanced resiliency and energy cost management.
**Value Proposition:** Guaranteed power reliability, optimized energy costs, and enhanced sustainability for critical operations.
**Customer Segments:** Non-Regulated Energy-Intensive Businesses.
**Delivery Model:** Long-term service contract, build-own-operate (BOO) model.
**Key Enabling L1 Capabilities:** Non-Regulated Product Development, Non-Regulated Sales & Marketing, Energy Generation, Energy Transmission, Electric Distribution, System Operations & Grid Control, Procurement & Supply Chain Management.

### 6.3. Grid Services Participation Program (DER Aggregation)

**Product ID:** NONREG-GRID-001
**Scope:** Non-Regulated (but interacts with regulated grid)
**Description:** Aggregation of customer-owned DERs (solar, storage, smart thermostats) into virtual power plants (VPPs) to provide grid services and participate in wholesale markets, offering financial incentives to customers.
**Value Proposition:** Earn revenue from customer-owned energy assets, contribute to grid stability, and support a cleaner energy system.
**Customer Segments:** Non-Regulated DG & Storage Adopters, Smart Home Customers.
**Delivery Model:** Enrollment-based, performance-based compensation.
**Key Enabling L1 Capabilities:** Non-Regulated Product Development, System Operations & Grid Control (DERMS), Metering and Measurement, Energy Trading and Risk Management, Customer Engagement & Service.
