## Executive Summary

This document defines the key performance metrics (KPMs) for Quantum Energy's diverse portfolio of regulated and non-regulated products and services. These KPMs are essential for tracking product health, evaluating success against strategic objectives, guiding product development, and informing marketing and sales strategies. Metrics are tailored to reflect the unique goals and constraints of each product's regulatory scope.

---

## 1. Introduction

Product performance metrics provide quantitative indicators of how well a product is meeting its objectives, satisfying customer needs, and contributing to Quantum Energy's overall financial and strategic goals. By monitoring these metrics, we can identify areas for improvement, assess market fit, and ensure our offerings remain competitive and valuable.
