## 2. Product Categories (Overview)

Quantum Energy's products and services are organized into seven categories to provide a clear structure for understanding our diverse offerings:

1.  **Core Regulated Electric Energy Products:** Foundational electric services under regulatory oversight.
2.  **Core Regulated Natural Gas Energy Products:** Foundational natural gas services under regulatory oversight.
3.  **Regulated Dual-Fuel Products:** Bundled regulated electric and natural gas services.
4.  **Non-Regulated Smart Home & Digital Solutions:** Products enhancing customer control, comfort, and energy management through smart technologies.
5.  **Non-Regulated EV & Transportation Solutions:** Products supporting the transition to electric transportation.
6.  **Non-Regulated Distributed Energy & Resiliency Solutions:** Products focusing on local energy generation, storage, and grid interaction.
7.  **Enterprise Advisory & Utility-Supporting Services:** Services leveraging Quantum Energy's expertise across both regulated and non-regulated contexts.
