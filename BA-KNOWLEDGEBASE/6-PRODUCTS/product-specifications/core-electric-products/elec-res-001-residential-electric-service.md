## Executive Summary

This document provides a detailed specification for Quantum Energy's Residential Electric Service (Product ID: ELEC-RES-001). This is a core regulated utility product, defining the standard electric energy delivery service provided to residential customers within our service territory. It outlines the product's features, value proposition, target segments, and the key capabilities required for its delivery.

---

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** Residential Electric Service
**Product ID:** ELEC-RES-001
**Regulatory Scope:** Regulated
**Description:** The provision of electric energy from Quantum Energy's distribution network to residential customers' premises for typical household uses. This includes the infrastructure to deliver power, metering, billing, and associated customer support services, all operating under state and federal regulatory frameworks.

---

## 2. Value Proposition

*   **Reliable Power:** Consistent and dependable supply of electricity to meet household needs.
*   **Safety Assurance:** Delivery through infrastructure maintained to high safety standards, with responsive emergency services.
*   **Affordability:** Fair and regulated pricing, ensuring access to essential energy.
*   **Convenience:** Easy access to account information, billing, and customer support.

---

## 3. Target Customer Segments

*   **Primary:** Regulated Residential Customers (Homeowners, Renters, Multi-family Dwelling Residents).

---

## 4. Product Features & Components

*   **Energy Delivery:** Provision of alternating current (AC) electricity at standard residential voltage (e.g., 120/240 volts).
*   **Distribution Infrastructure:** Connection to Quantum Energy's electric distribution network.
*   **Metering:** Installation and maintenance of an electric meter to measure consumption.
*   **Billing:** Monthly invoicing for energy consumed and associated charges based on approved tariffs.
*   **Outage Response:** 24/7 service for reporting and restoring power outages.
*   **Customer Support:** Access to Quantum Energy's customer service channels (phone, digital) for inquiries and assistance.
*   **Safety Services:** Emergency response for electrical hazards.
*   **Power Quality:** Provision of electricity within acceptable voltage and frequency tolerances.

---

## 5. Delivery Model

*   **Model Type:** Continuous Service.
*   **Contract Term:** Indefinite (as long as customer resides at service address).
*   **Billing Cycle:** Monthly.
*   **Payment Options:** Direct debit, online payment, mail, phone, in-person.
*   **Service Activation:** Typically within 1-2 business days for standard connections.

---

## 6. Rate Structures (Examples)

*   **Standard Residential Rate:** Per-kilowatt-hour ($/kWh) charge, potentially with a fixed customer charge.
*   **Tiered Rates:** Charges vary based on consumption blocks (e.g., higher rates for higher usage).
*   **Time-of-Use (TOU) Rates:** Charges vary based on time of day and season.
*   **Budget Billing:** Optional program to levelize monthly payments.
