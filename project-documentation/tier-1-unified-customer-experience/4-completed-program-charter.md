# Program Charter

## Program Title: Unified Customer Experience (UCX) Program

- **Program ID:** PGM-00012
- **Demand ID:** DEM-00175
- **Executive Sponsor:** Isabella Rossi (Chief Customer Officer)
- **Program Director:** TBD (Assigned by EPMO)

### 1. Program Vision & Strategic Alignment
This program will transform our customer experience by creating a single, unified platform for all customer interactions. It is the primary vehicle for achieving the corporate strategic objective: "Deliver a World-Class, Digitally-Enabled Customer Experience." The program will deliver a seamless journey for our customers and empower our employees with a 360-degree view of the people we serve.

### 2. Program Scope & Key Deliverables
This is a multi-year program to replace our fragmented CRM and portal landscape.

#### Key Deliverables:
- A new enterprise-wide CRM platform.
- A unified, responsive customer web portal.
- A native customer mobile application (iOS and Android).
- A "Customer 360" data platform.
- A comprehensive library of reusable business capability APIs.
- Redesigned and streamlined customer journeys for core interactions.

### 3. Program Roadmap & Timeline
- **Total Duration:** 3 Years
- **Year 1: Foundation:** Deliver new Agent Desktop (CRM) and migrate residential customers.
- **Year 2: Digital Experience:** Launch new Customer Portal & Mobile App. Migrate commercial customers.
- **Year 3: Innovation:** Deliver personalization, analytics, and new value-added services.

### 4. Program Budget
- **Total Approved Budget:** $18,000,000
- **Year 1 Funding Release:** $6,000,000
- **Funding Type:** Strategic Initiative Capital

### 5. Program Governance & Organization
- **Executive Sponsor:** Isabella Rossi
- **Steering Committee:** The Technology Advisory Committee (TAC) will serve as the steering committee, meeting quarterly to review program progress, budget, and risks.
- **Program Director:** A dedicated Program Director will be hired to lead the program full-time.
- **Workstreams:** The program will be organized into the following workstreams, each with a dedicated lead:
    - Platform & Architecture
    - CRM & Agent Desktop
    - Digital Experience (Portal & Mobile)
    - Data & Integration
    - Organizational Change Management (OCM)

### 6. Success Metrics & Benefits Realization
- **Primary Metrics:**
    - Increase Net Promoter Score (NPS) by 20 points by end of Year 3.
    - Achieve $5M in annualized operational savings and new revenue by end of Year 3.
- **Benefits Realization:** The EPMO will track and report on these metrics to the steering committee on a semi-annual basis.
