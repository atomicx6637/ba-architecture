# Project Charter

## Project Title: Core Middleware Platform Upgrade

- **Project ID:** PRJ-00452
- **Demand ID:** DEM-00174
- **Project Sponsor:** Maria Rodriguez (VP, IT Platforms)
- **Project Manager:** TBD (Assigned by EPMO)

### 1. Project Overview & Goals
This project will mitigate critical business risk by upgrading the end-of-life MessageBus v3.1 platform to the fully supported MessageBus v4.5. The goals are to ensure business continuity, improve security, enhance performance, and establish a scalable foundation for future integrations.

### 2. Scope
#### In Scope:
- Procurement and installation of new hardware and software.
- Migration of all existing integrations for CRM, Billing, ERP, and Marketing systems.
- Creation of a parallel test environment.
- Full end-to-end performance and regression testing.
- A planned production cutover event.
- Decommissioning of all v3.1 hardware and software.

#### Out of Scope:
- Re-architecting business processes within the connected applications.
- Adding new integrations not currently on the v3.1 platform.

### 3. Key Milestones & Timeline
- **Phase 1 (6 months):** Procurement, Installation, and Test Environment Setup.
- **Phase 2 (4 months):** Integration Migration & Performance Testing.
- **Phase 3 (2 months):** Production Cutover, Decommissioning, and Project Closeout.
- **Estimated Total Duration:** 12 Months

### 4. Budget
- **Approved Budget:** $1,200,000
- **Breakdown:**
    - CapEx (Hardware/Software): $700,000
    - OpEx (Services/Internal Resources): $500,000
- **Contingency:** 15% ($180,000)

### 5. Project Team & Governance
- **Sponsor:** Maria Rodriguez
- **Project Manager:** TBD
- **Lead Architect:** Frank Lee
- **Steering Committee:** Technology Subcommittee, with participation from Directors of CRM, Finance, and Marketing.
- **Key Stakeholders:** Application owners of all connected systems.

### 6. Success Criteria
- All integrations migrated successfully with zero data loss.
- The new platform demonstrates a 25% improvement in peak-hour transaction throughput.
- Zero security vulnerabilities rated "High" or "Critical" in the new platform.
- The old MessageBus v3.1 platform is fully decommissioned within 30 days of go-live.
